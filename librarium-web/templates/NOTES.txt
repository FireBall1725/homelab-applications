The web image's bundled nginx.conf proxies /api and /health to
http://librarium-api:8080 by the bare service name, so librarium-web must be
deployed into the same namespace as librarium-api (app-librarium).

Access the UI:
  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "common.names.fullname" . }} 3000:3000

Then open http://localhost:3000
